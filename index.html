About Me

Over the past few years, I have gone though a significant career transition from being a bartender to becoming a software developer. During this period I was finally given the opportunity to learn a skill that I've been interested in learning for years. Because of this life changing opportunity, I discovered my passion for learning and writing code, which I find fulfilling and exciting. When I'm not on my computer I enjoy spending time with my family and exploring the great outdoors. A few of my many hobbies are scuba diving, camping, fishing, snowboarding, and playing video games. These activities help me stay active and continue to appreciate the beauty of nature. Overall, I'm a diligent methodical worker who is constantly striving to expand my knowledge and skills.
